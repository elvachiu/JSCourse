Rules

Keep the ball in the black box.

How to control the ball:

  • press arrow left to go left
  • press arrow right to go right

Game over when the ball:

  • falls into lava (the yellow part)
  • goes above the sky (out of the black box)
  • hit by the bullets (coming from the right)

Different stair types:

  • normal (blue): no special effect
  • flip (green): directly go through
  • scrolling (gray): scroll to the left

Special power & bonus:

  • Press key c: generate one stair somewhere below the ball (can only use 5 times)
  • The golden coin: plus 10 points
